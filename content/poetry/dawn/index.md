Empty promises

Broken ties

Everything she misses

Well here it lies.

Thirteen feet under the ground 

Safe and sound

That's where she was found

Tethered and bound.

Everything meaningless 

Begging to be blessed 

No more no less

Just remained a fractured mess.

Nails hammered down

The coffin so brown

In the tainted white gown

Bloomed a new dark dawn.
